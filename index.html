    About Us

    Make India Proud Is MY FIRST OFFICE INITIATIVE BY Student-driven NON PROFITABLE ORGANISATION that mainly strives for increasing innovations through science and technology. WE ARE BUILDING A structure for ideas and innovations in the SOCIETY and an opportunity for the INDIANS to make a change in their way of approach over the current societal needs of the WORLD. We want MIP to be the Voice of students in India.

    VISION

    Our Vision is to make Our Country Proud through Science And Technology .we want to establish product base companies in India by Indians.

    MISSION

    Our mission is to establish a Knowledge sharing Student Community. We wish to launch a full-scale Crowd Funding App in which every student with a creative and unique Idea gets funded if it is proven worth it at least by one of the persons, in the Board members of MIP.
